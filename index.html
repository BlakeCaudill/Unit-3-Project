Left Behind

You wake up in a zombie infested city realizing that you have been left behind and your city is crubling. The zombie population is multiplying in numbers, you know you half to escape if you have any chance to live. QUICK, you must make this life or death decision, go left or go right, chose wisely...

You chose left. you find a working motorcycle and escape the city

You chose right. You go that way and before you know it, you are surrounded by hundreds zombies

You finally get to the outskirts of the city with plenty of gas to spare. YOU SURVIVE!

You sadly did not make it out of the city and you will now live the rest of your life a zombie. YOU LOSE!
